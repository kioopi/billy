Help

Loading an original Image:

  • Find a picture in the internet you want to billyfy.
  • Right-Click on the picture and choose "Copy image URL" or "Copy Adress"
  • Come back here and paste the URL in the text-field.
  • Click "Load" (and wait for a couple of seconds)

Or:

  • Drag an image from your computer and drop it in the workspace.

Editing:

  • Move or resize the image with the mouse until it fits on the working-space.
  • Add a Billyface (or several) by clicking it on the left sidebar.
  • Move, rotate or resize it using the mouse.

Saving:

  • Click the "Save" Button.
  • A new Tab is opened, showing the new image.
  • Click it with the right mouse-button and choose "Save image as..."
  • Go to The Appreciation Society and post your contribution.

This tool is a contribution to the The Billiam Van Kiddur Appreciation Society, a meeting point for those whose lives would be empty without Billiam Van Kiddur, in order to share information, photographs and other tidbits to give our lives meaning.

The idea is to aide the process of democratizing the creation of billyfied images by making tools accessible outside of the boundaries of the club that is photoshop owners.

If you have questions, suggestions or found bugs and error, please don't hesitate to contact me at kioopi@gmail.com / @kioopi. The source-code of this application is available at github.com.
