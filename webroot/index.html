Classic Billyfication

  • Find a picture you want to billyfy.
  • Right-Click on the picture and choose "Copy image URL"
  • Go back to cbf and paste the URL in the text-field.
  • Click "Load Image" (and wait for a couple of seconds)
  • Move or resize the image with the mouse until it fits on the working-space.
  • Add a Billyface (or several) by clicking it on the left sidebar.
  • Move, rotate or resize it using the mouse.
  • Click the "Save" Button.
  • A new Tab is opened, showing the new image.
  • Click it with the right mouse-button and choose "Save image as..."
  • Go to The Appreciation Society and post your contribution.
